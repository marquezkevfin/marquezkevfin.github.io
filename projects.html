Portfolio Construction Project

Taking a Portfolio Management course in my fourth year provided me with an encompassing knowledge of the discipline of portfolio management including minimizing risk, maximizing risk-adjusted returns, constructing a portfolio and benchmark, and analyzing the source of returns. All of this knowledge was put into practical use for our final project which involved constructing a portfolio of 15 stocks, creating a benchmark in which we compare our portfolio returns against, and using 2-factor brinson attribution to analyze the source of excess returns. We were also provided with Excel templates that will allow us to replicate these steps and construct different portfolios.
