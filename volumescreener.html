Google Finance SP500 & R1K Volume Screener Tool

Dashboard page for Google Finance Volume Screener Tool

This page displays relevant data of stocks listed in the S&P500.

The function =Average(index(GOOGLEFINANCE($A2,"volume",WORKDAY(today(),-5),today()-1),,2)) calculates the average volume of the previous 5 trading days. The vol/volavg(5td) divides the volume of the current day to the average volume of the 5 previous trading days and gives a ratio.

Data filter page for S&P500.

The function =FILTER('SP500' !A:A, 'SP500' !$K:$K>=1.9) selects the stocks in the SP500 page that has a vol/volavg greater than 1.9. In other words, the function selects stocks that has seen a sudden spike in volume compared to the volume for the five previous trading days.
